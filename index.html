How It Works

Orion Dev Core runs your front office in five simple steps.

  1. Lead says hello
    Prospects arrive via your website, WhatsApp Business, or campaign links. Orion greets them with a friendly, human‑like tone—no botspeak.
  2. Orion builds rapport and qualifies
    Orion chats naturally (rugby scores, local weather, simple small talk) to make people comfortable, then asks smart questions to qualify each lead and capture accurate details. It handles objections with empathy and keeps the conversation moving toward a clear next step.
  3. Smart workflows handle the admin
    Behind the scenes, Orion writes to your Google Sheets, sends confirmations, and updates your systems. Each interaction generates structured data you can use for reporting and follow‑ups.
  4. Calendar and payments on autopilot
    Orion books appointments directly into your calendar and triggers Yoco payment links for deposits or full payments, supporting major cards and digital wallets where available.
  5. You just show up
    By the time you meet a client, Orion has already captured their details, answered basic questions, and scheduled the meeting. You focus on delivering the service; Orion handles reception, admin, and follow‑through.

Powered by a multi‑AI stack

Behind the scenes, Orion uses a combination of Gemini (for conversational intelligence and context), Perplexity Pro (for live research and structured answers), and Claude Pro (for reasoning and long‑form content). This gives your office manager the equivalent of a small AI team working in sync.
